What is CSS?

Cascading Style Sheets (CSS) is used to format the layout of a webpage.

With CSS, you can control the color,font, the size of text, the spacting between elements, how elements are positioned and laid out, what
background images ỏ background colors are to be use, different diserent devices and screen size, and much more!

Tip: the word cascading means that style applied to a parent element will also apply to all children elements within the paren.
